The ingredients needed to make Mojo Pork Ribs:

  • Get 4 country-style pork ribs, rinsed, or more as needed
  • Take 1 dash seasoned salt, or to taste
  • Make ready 1 dash garlic powder, or to taste
  • Get ground black pepper to taste
  • Take 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Take ½ yellow onion, cut into wedges
  • Prepare ¼ cup fresh cilantro, or to taste
  • Make ready 1 cup mojo (citrus) marinade
  • Get 1 cup chicken broth
  • Get 1 splash orange juice

Step by Step to create Mojo Pork Ribs:

  1. Season ribs with seasoned salt, garlic powder, and black pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 5 minutes. Add cilantro. Add ribs; cook until browned on 1 side, about 3 minutes. Turn and cook until other side is browned, 2 to 3 minutes more. Pour in enough marinade to coat ribs; continue to brown, about 3 minutes more. Add chicken broth and orange juice to cover ribs.
  3. Close cooker securely and place pressure regulator over vent according to manufacturer's instructions. Heat until steam escapes in a steady flow, about 5 minutes. Reduce pressure and cook until ribs are tender, 20 to 25 minutes. Let pressure release naturally according to manufacturer's instructions, 5 to 10 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid.
