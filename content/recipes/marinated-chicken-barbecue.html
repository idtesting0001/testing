The ingredients needed to make Marinated Chicken Barbecue:

  • Take 8 breast half, bone and skin removed (blank)s skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Take 1 cup ketchup
  • Get 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • Make ready ¼ cup lemon juice
  • Prepare ½ cup vegetable oil

Steps to create Marinated Chicken Barbecue:

  1. Mix together ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, and oil.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a dish, and pour marinade over. Refrigerate overnight.
