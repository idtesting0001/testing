Simple Way to Prepare Ultimate Chocolate Fudge Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Chocolate Fudge using 4 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Fudge:

  • Take 3 cups white sugar
  • Make ready 1 cup evaporated milk
  • Get ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Get ¼ cup creamy peanut butter

Instructions to create Chocolate Fudge:

  1. In a 3 quart saucepan, combine white sugar, evaporated milk, and cocoa. Bring to a hard boil, and then reduce heat to medium. Continue cooking until it reaches the soft ball stage, 234 degrees F (112 degrees C).
  2. Stir in peanut butter until well blended. Pour mixture into a buttered 8x8 inch baking dish. Cool, and cut into pieces.
