How to Make Award-winning Portobello Steaks Delicious

To begin with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Portobello Steaks using 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Portobello Steaks:

  • Prepare 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • Get 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Get 3 tablespoons vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Get 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Take 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Take salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Make ready cooking spray

Steps to make Portobello Steaks:

  1. Remove stems and clean the mushrooms; set aside.
  2. Mix vinegar, Worcestershire, oil, garlic, onion powder, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Place mushrooms inside a large zip-top bag. Pour in the marinade and turn the bag so the mushrooms get evenly coated. Let sit in a refrigerator for a minimum of 5 minutes, or up to 12 hours.
  4. Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over high heat. Grill mushrooms in the hot pan until heated through and browned, about 4 minutes per side.
