Simple Way to Prepare Homemade Keto Zaatar Buns Tasty

To begin with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Keto Zaatar Buns using 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Keto Zaatar Buns:

  • Make ready 1 tablespoon butter
  • Take 1 cup almond flour
  • Get ¼ cup fresh za'atar
  • Get 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Get 3 eggs
  • Take 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • Take ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon salt

Steps to create Keto Zaatar Buns:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter 8 muffin cups.
  2. Mix almond flour, za'atar, olive oil, eggs, yogurt, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.
  3. Divide batter evenly into the prepared muffin cups using an ice cream scoop.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
