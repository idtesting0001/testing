The ingredients needed to make Blackberry-Cucumber Vodka Tonic:

  • Make ready 4 slices cucumber
  • Prepare 5 fresh blackberries
  • Make ready 1 cup crushed ice
  • Get 1 ½ ounces vodka
  • Prepare 3 ounces tonic water

Instructions to make Blackberry-Cucumber Vodka Tonic:

  1. Muddle 3 slices of cucumber and 4 blackberries in the bottom of a rocks glass. Fill glass with crushed ice and add vodka. Top with tonic water and stir to combine. Garnish with remaining slice of cucumber and blackberry.
