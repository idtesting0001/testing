The ingredients needed to make Green Pea and Ham Salad:

  • Make ready 1 (10 ounce) package frozen peas, thawed and drained
  • Take 2 cups cubed fully cooked ham
  • Get 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Get ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • Get 2 tablespoons onion, chopped
  • Get 1 ½ teaspoons mustard

Instructions to make Green Pea and Ham Salad:

  1. Mix peas, ham, Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, onion, and Dijon mustard in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
