The ingredients needed to make Pasta Fruit Salad I:

  • Get 1 pound rotini pasta
  • Make ready 1 (15 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
  • Take 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
  • Take ¼ cup chopped green onions
  • Take 1 cup diced ham
  • Make ready ½ cup creamy salad dressing

Step by Step to cook Pasta Fruit Salad I:

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. In a salad bowl, combine the pasta, oranges, pineapple, green onions, ham and creamy salad dressing. Mix well and chill before serving.
