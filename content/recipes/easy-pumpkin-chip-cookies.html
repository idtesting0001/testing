Easiest Way to Prepare Speedy Easy Pumpkin Chip Cookies Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Easy Pumpkin Chip Cookies using 12 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Easy Pumpkin Chip Cookies:

  • Prepare 2 cups white sugar
  • Take 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • Make ready 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Take 2 eggs
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons milk
  • Get 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Make ready 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Make ready 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • Prepare 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • Take 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  • Make ready 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions to create Easy Pumpkin Chip Cookies:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease two baking sheets.
  2. Whisk sugar, pumpkin, vegetable oil, eggs, milk, and vanilla together in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Stir flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl. Gradually pour flour mixture into pumpkin mixture; stir to incorporate. Stir chocolate chips into dough. Drop spoonfuls of the dough 2 inches apart onto prepared baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 10 minutes.
