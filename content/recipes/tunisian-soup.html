The ingredients needed to make Tunisian Soup:

  • Get 2 tablespoons butter
  • Take 1 onion, chopped
  • Prepare 1 cup couscous
  • Make ready 8 cups water
  • Prepare 2 zucchini, chopped
  • Prepare 1 potato, chopped
  • Prepare 20 green beans, chopped
  • Make ready 2 carrots, chopped
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato puree
  • Prepare ½ teaspoon ground coriander
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon red chile powder
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • Make ready ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Take ½ cup milk

Step by Step to create Tunisian Soup:

  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes. Mix in couscous; cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in water, zucchini, potato, green beans, carrots, tomato puree, coriander, chile powder, cumin, and salt; bring to a boil. Simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Stir in milk.
