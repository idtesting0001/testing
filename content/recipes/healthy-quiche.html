Simple Way to Cook Ultimate Healthy Quiche Tasty

To begin with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can have Healthy Quiche using 11 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Healthy Quiche:

  • Take cooking spray
  • Make ready ¾ cup chopped onion
  • Make ready ½ cup chopped zucchini
  • Prepare ¾ cup chopped cauliflower
  • Make ready 2 tablespoons water
  • Take 10 large egg whites
  • Make ready ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • Take ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Take ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Prepare ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Make ready ¼ cup shredded pepperjack cheese

Steps to cook Healthy Quiche:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Coat a skillet with cooking spray and place over medium heat; cook and stir onion and zucchini until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Place cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and add water; cook in microwave until cauliflower is tender, about 3 minutes. Drain water.
  4. Whisk egg whites, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; add zucchini-onion mixture, cauliflower, and pepperjack cheese. Pour egg mixture into a 10-inch round pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until quiche is set in the middle, about 25 minutes.
