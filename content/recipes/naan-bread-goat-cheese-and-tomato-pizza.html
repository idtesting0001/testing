To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook Naan Bread Goat Cheese and Tomato Pizza using 6 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Naan Bread Goat Cheese and Tomato Pizza:

  • Take 1 naan bread
  • Take 2 tablespoons Caesar salad dressing
  • Take 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • Prepare ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning, or to taste
  • Make ready 4 slices cooked bacon, chopped
  • Get 2 tablespoons crumbled goat cheese, or to taste

Instructions to create Naan Bread Goat Cheese and Tomato Pizza:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place naan bread on a baking sheet. Brush naan with Caesar salad dressing. Spread tomato slices over dressing. Sprinkle Italian seasoning on top. Arrange bacon on top. Sprinkle goat cheese over bacon.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is bubbling, 10 to 12 minutes. Turn on the broiler; broil pizza until goat cheese is lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
