Recipe of Quick Ultimate Turkey Brine Delicious

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Ultimate Turkey Brine using 8 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Ultimate Turkey Brine:

  • Get 1 gallon water
  • Prepare 2 cups kosher salt
  • Take 1 ½ cups dark brown sugar
  • Make ready 12 whole cloves
  • Get 3 bay leaves
  • Prepare 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • Get 2 ½ teaspoons dried rosemary
  • Make ready 2 ½ teaspoons dried thyme

Steps to create Ultimate Turkey Brine:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large stockpot. Stir salt and sugar into the water until dissolved; bring water again to a boil. Add cloves, bay leaves, peppercorns, rosemary, and thyme to the water, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook mixture at a simmer for about 20 minutes.
  2. Refrigerate brine until completely cooled.
