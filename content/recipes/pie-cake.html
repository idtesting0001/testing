To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can cook Pie Cake using 9 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Pie Cake:

  • Take 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • Prepare 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  • Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Make ready 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Get 3 eggs, beaten
  • Take 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • Make ready 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps to cook Pie Cake:

  1. Grease and flour a 9 x 13 inch pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C ).
  2. Toss sugar, sifted flour, salt, soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl.
  3. Fold in oil, eggs, chopped nuts, and pie filling. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  4. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until cake tests done.
