Steps to Prepare Any-night-of-the-week Nan Mays Snowballs Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Nan Mays Snowballs using 7 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Nan Mays Snowballs:

  • Take 1 cup 2% milk
  • Get 1 cup white sugar
  • Get ¼ cup butter
  • Make ready ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Make ready 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Make ready 2 cups rolled oats
  • Make ready 2 ½ cups unsweetened shredded coconut, divided

Step by Step to cook Nan Mays Snowballs:

  1. Combine milk, sugar, butter, and cocoa powder in a large pot; bring to a boil for 3 minutes. Stir vanilla extract into the milk mixture; boil another 2 minutes. Remove pot from heat.
  2. Stir oats and 2 cups coconut into the milk mixture until thoroughly mixed; cool for 5 minutes.
  3. Spread 1/2 cup coconut onto a plate. Roll the mixture into 24 balls and roll in the coconut. Set on a sheet of parchment paper until finished.
