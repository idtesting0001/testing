Simple Way to Prepare Award-winning Texas Chocolate Mini Cake Bites Tasty

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Texas Chocolate Mini Cake Bites using 9 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Texas Chocolate Mini Cake Bites:

  • Make ready cooking spray
  • Get 1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
  • Take 4 eggs
  • Take 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate fudge pudding mix
  • Prepare ½ cup vegetable oil
  • Get ¼ cup water
  • Make ready 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • Make ready ¼ cup water
  • Take 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Step by Step to cook Texas Chocolate Mini Cake Bites:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 2 mini muffin tins generously with cooking spray.
  2. Combine devil's food cake mix, eggs, chocolate pudding mix, vegetable oil, and 1/4 cup water in a large bowl. Mix with a wooden spoon until batter is thick and smooth.
  3. Pour batter into the prepared mini muffin tins.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 9 to 10 minutes.
  5. Let mini cakes cool in the tins for 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack.
  6. Stir confectioners' sugar, 1/4 cup water, and cocoa powder together in a bowl to make glaze.
  7. Dip tops of the mini cakes in glaze and place on a large sheet of waxed paper. Cool completely before serving, about 10 minutes.
