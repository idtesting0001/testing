The ingredients needed to make Smoked Turkey Tortilla Wraps:

  • Make ready 10 whole wheat flour tortillas
  • Take 10 slices smoked turkey, cut into thin strips
  • Take 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
  • Prepare ½ cup sour cream, for topping
  • Make ready ½ cup Cheddar cheese

Steps to make Smoked Turkey Tortilla Wraps:

  1. Heat flour tortillas in a large skillet over medium heat until slightly browned. Divide turkey strips, avocado, sour cream, and cheese among the warmed tortillas. Fold in half and serve.
