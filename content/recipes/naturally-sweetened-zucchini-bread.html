To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Naturally Sweetened Zucchini Bread using 7 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Naturally Sweetened Zucchini Bread:

  • Make ready 1 tablespoon flour, or as needed for dusting
  • Take 1 ½ cups honey
  • Prepare 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Prepare 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Make ready 4 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • Take 1 teaspoon salt
  • Take 2 cups grated zucchini

Instructions to make Naturally Sweetened Zucchini Bread:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a loaf pan and dust with flour.
  2. Mix honey and vanilla extract together in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a separate bowl; stir into honey mixture in small portions, integrating each before adding the next. Fold zucchini into batter. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool in the pans 5 to 10 minutes before cutting to serve.
