To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Hannahs Peanut Butter Oatmeal using 7 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Hannahs Peanut Butter Oatmeal:

  • Take 1 cup water
  • Prepare ½ cup whole oats
  • Make ready 1 dash salt
  • Take 1 tablespoon peanut butter, or to taste
  • Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Take 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • Take ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Step by Step to cook Hannahs Peanut Butter Oatmeal:

  1. Combine water and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Stir in oats and cook, stirring, for 5 to 6 minutes.
  2. Scoop up all of the oatmeal and transfer into a small bowl. Stir in peanut butter until well combined. Use a butter knife to help mix it in if necessary. Mix in vanilla extract. Combine sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over the top.
