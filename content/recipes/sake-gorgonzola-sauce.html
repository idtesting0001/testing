To begin with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Sake Gorgonzola Sauce using 9 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Sake Gorgonzola Sauce:

  • Take ⅓ cup sake
  • Get 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Make ready 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Get ⅓ cup heavy whipping cream
  • Get 2 teaspoons basil
  • Prepare 2 teaspoons parsley
  • Prepare salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Take ¼ cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
  • Take 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to make Sake Gorgonzola Sauce:

  1. Combine sake, butter, and garlic into a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until the mixture has thickened and reduced in volume by one half, about 3 minutes. Add cream to the sake mixture; stir until reduced by one fourth, about 3 minutes. Season with basil, parsley, and salt and pepper.
  2. Stir Gorgonzola cheese and Parmesan cheese slowly into the sake mixture; simmer and stir continuously until smooth, about 3 minutes. Simmer until flavors combine, about 5 minutes.
