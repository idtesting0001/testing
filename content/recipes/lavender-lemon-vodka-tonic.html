The ingredients needed to make Lavender-Lemon Vodka Tonic:

  • Get 2 sprigs fresh lavender, lightly squeezed
  • Take 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
  • Prepare 1 wedge fresh lemon
  • Prepare 4 cubes ice, or as needed
  • Prepare 1 ½ fluid ounces tonic water, or more to taste

Instructions to cook Lavender-Lemon Vodka Tonic:

  1. Put lavender sprigs in cocktail glass; add vodka. Refrigerate vodka mixture until lavender is infused into vodka, about 10 minutes.
  2. Squeeze lemon over vodka; add ice cubes and tonic water.
