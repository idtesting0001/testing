The ingredients needed to make Marthas Hot Almond Spread:

  • Get 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • Prepare ⅓ cup mayonnaise
  • Get 3 green onions, chopped
  • Make ready 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • Get 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • Get 8 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
  • Get ¼ cup sliced almonds

Step by Step to cook Marthas Hot Almond Spread:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the cream cheese, mayonnaise, onions, pepper and nutmeg. Stir in the Swiss cheese. Transfer to small ovenproof glass bowl. Sprinkle 1/2 of the almonds on top.
  3. Bake for 7 minutes. Stir, and bake for an additional 8 minutes. Sprinkle remaining almonds on top, and serve.
